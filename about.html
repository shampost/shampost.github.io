About Me

Cristian Campos is a designer and data scientist based in Raleigh, NC. Currently, he works at HTC Global Services as an Escalation Specialist, where he routes high, medium, and standard priority help desk tickets for fast and effective resolutions. 

His passion is about making life easier and more enjoyable, whether through creating beautiful designs or making data work for social good. 

He is a tech enthusiast with more than 5 years of experience turning client briefs into quality products. 

He began his early career at the age of 17 as a facilitator at a non-profit organization, where he trained young people and adults on the power of youth advocacy and racial equity. Today, he volunteers his data skills at various non-profits to optimize data analysis, pipelines, and management.
